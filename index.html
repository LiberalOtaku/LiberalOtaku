Landed an Internship . . . But Still Seeking Employment!

Last Updated: 11 August 2017

Last month, I finally started my first software engineering internship at We Vote, a non-profit organization that is developing a social network that aims to make ballot information more accessible to all citizens. I have been having a lot of fun with it so far as it gave me a chance to learn React and Sass/SCSS for the first time and get some hands-on experience with both of them. Moreover, I get to work on an app that aims to promote social good as well. See my GitHub profile for the latest contributions.

The only downside, however, is that the position is unpaid, making it less of an internship and more of a volunteer opportunity. Nevertheless, it is something that I can add to my resume and use to find a paid job opportunity (hopefully) soon. My job hunting still continues! See my next post for details.
